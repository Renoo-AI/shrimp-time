# Shrimp Time Asset Requirements

To execute the brand identity flawlessly based on the logo analysis, the following assets are required:

## 1. Photography
- **Vibe:** Nautical luxury, vibrant coastal elements.
- **Lighting:** High-contrast maritime lighting, emphasizing the freshness of seafood against deep navy (#012f51) and bright yellow accents. NO heavy chiaroscuro/dark shadows unless stylized.

## 2. Typography Licenses
- **Playfair Display:** Standard Google Fonts usage.
- **Inter:** Standard Google Fonts usage.

## 3. Vector Assets
- **Logo:** High-resolution transparent SVG version of the Shrimp Time logo matching the exact structure from the provided image.

## 4. UI Textures
- **Backgrounds:** Smooth, high-res gradients using the official `#012f51` and `#F5D300` brand colors.
